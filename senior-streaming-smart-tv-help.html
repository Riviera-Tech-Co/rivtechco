Streaming, Smart TV & Sonos Help

Set up your shows and speakers, once, so you're not fighting three remotes (or a phone app) every night.

Switching from cable to streaming, getting a new smart TV set up right, or adding Sonos speakers to a room or the whole house — shouldn't mean juggling three remotes and guessing which app to open. We'll set it up once, clearly labeled, so it just works.

What's Included

  • Smart TV and streaming device setup (Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, built-in smart TV apps)
  • Setting up Netflix, YouTube, Prime Video, and other services you already pay for
  • Simplifying remotes — including universal remote setup if you're tired of juggling several
  • Cutting the cord: moving from cable to streaming without losing the channels you actually watch
  • Sonos and multi-room audio setup — connecting speakers to your Wi-Fi, grouping rooms, and linking Spotify, Apple Music, or other services (wireless setup only — no in-wall wiring or electrical work)
  • Troubleshooting "the TV won't connect" and "I can't find my show" problems
  • Written, step-by-step instructions left with you for the basics (turn on, change input, find a show)

How It Works

One visit typically covers a full setup. If you're deciding whether to cut cable altogether, we can also just talk through the options and costs — no pressure either way.
