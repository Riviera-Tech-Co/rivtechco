Password, Privacy & Scam Protection

Protect your accounts and your money — and learn to spot the scams before they cost you anything.

Scams targeting seniors keep getting more convincing — fake tech support calls, phishing emails, texts claiming to be from your bank. We help you lock down your accounts and, just as importantly, build the confidence to recognize what's real and what isn't.

What's Included

  • Setting up strong, manageable passwords (and a safe way to keep track of them)
  • Reviewing your accounts for signs of a breach or suspicious activity
  • Walking through real examples of scam calls, texts, and emails so you know what to watch for
  • Setting up two-factor authentication on accounts that support it
  • Removing remote-access software if you've been targeted by a fake "tech support" caller
  • A judgment-free conversation if you think you may have already been scammed — the priority is fixing it, not blame

How It Works

If you think an account has already been compromised or money has been taken, call us right away — we'll walk you through immediate steps. Otherwise, a single visit is usually enough to review your accounts and get everything secured.
