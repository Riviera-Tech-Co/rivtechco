Wi-Fi, Email & Internet Setup

Get connected — and stay connected — without the runaround.

Slow Wi-Fi, an email inbox that's stopped working, or a router that needs resetting every other day — these are some of the most common (and most frustrating) tech problems for anyone, and they're what we fix most often for senior clients.

What's Included

  • Home Wi-Fi setup, troubleshooting, and coverage improvements
  • Email account setup and recovery (Gmail, Yahoo, AOL, and others)
  • Fixing "I can't log in" and "I forgot my password" problems, safely
  • Setting up email on your phone, tablet, and computer so they all stay in sync
  • Organizing your inbox so important messages don't get lost or missed
  • Explaining what's spam, what's safe to open, and what to delete

How It Works

Most Wi-Fi and email issues can be fixed in a single visit. We'll also leave you with simple, written steps for anything you might need to do again on your own — no pressure to remember everything the first time.
